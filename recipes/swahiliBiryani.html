Ingredients

  • Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 5 chopped tomatoes
  • 6 onions sliced and deep fried until crispy
  • 70g tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp ground cinammon
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp crushed ginger
  • 1 tsp crushed garlic
  • Chopped coriander
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream

Steps

  1. In a pan add 2tbsp vegetable oil followed by the tomato paste and fry it for 1 min on medium heat.
  2. Add cumin, ground cinnamon, ground cardamom, black pepper and ground cloves
  3. Cook the spices for 2 min then add the chopped tomatoes and cover the pan.
  4. After 13 min, add the chicken, garlic, ginger and salt.
  5. Cover and cook until chicken soften (about 15min)
