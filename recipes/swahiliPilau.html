Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice(soaked)
  • 250g Beef boiled
  • 1/4 tsp Cumin
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp Cloves
  • 1/4 tsp Black peppercorns
  • Cinammon sticks
  • 2 Onions diced/sliced
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic crushed
  • 3 1/2 cups Beef broth

Steps

  1. Toast the spices on an ungreased pan until fragrant. Then grind the spices
  2. In a large pot, add some Vegetable oil and chopped onions. Youâ€™ll need to fry the onions until brown.
  3. Add the garlic. Once fragrant, add the boiled beef, spices and stir
  4. Add the rice and keep stirring. Add in the beef broth at this point. If it's not enough top it up wth water.
  5. Cover and let it cook on medium heat until there is no more liquid
  6. Serve with Kachumbari
