Kachumbari

Kachumbari is a fresh tomato and onion salad dish that is popular in the cuisines of the African Great Lakes region. It is an uncooked salad dish consisting of chopped tomatoes, onions, and chili peppers.

It is used as a side dish for a main meal such as Nyama Choma or Pilau


Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 Onions
  • Coriander bunch
  • 2 lemons
  • Salt
  • Vinegar (optional)
  • Chili pepper(optional)

Steps

  1. Clean the tomatoes and chop into small size pieces. Set aside,dice the onions finely chop the coriander slice the lemons into halves.
  2. Place all the ingredients in one bowl mix using a spoon.squeeze the lemon juice into your kachumbari and mix again add a pinch of salt to taste and the vinegar.
  3. Serve with main dish of choice
